about us

we are the comox valley folk socitey an non-profit organization formed in 1995 to produce vancouver island musicfest. we are working hard to improve who we are and how we do things in regard to quaility of music we present and how everyone is treated at our festival. We remain proud of our ecnomic impact as one of the primary drivers of cultural tourism on vancouver island
