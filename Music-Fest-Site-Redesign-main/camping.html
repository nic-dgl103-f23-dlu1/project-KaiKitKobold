steps needed for on-site camping

  1. purchase weekend pass. you must have a weekend pass to camp.
  2. purchase camping pass
  3. a link to the camping registration will be emailed to you with your camping tickets. please follow the instuctions on the registration form and complete all the info required. you cannot register until you have purchased a camping pass.
  4. read all the rules and regulations on this page before proceeding.

why you should camp with us

one of the best ways to truly experience vancouver island musicfest is to fully immerse yourself in the weekend through camping on-site. familes and friends flock from the local community and far away for an annual reunion of camping and festival fun. our venue is situated on a pastoral acreage bordering the forested banks of the tsoulm river. we provide camping opprotunites for rv's, campers, and camper vans and tents. this 'rustic camping' environment welcomes festival-goers and gentle jammers who are willing to be respectful, peaceful, and neighbourly. camping is open to festival goers who are willing to engage in a camping community where mutual respect, caring, and calm prevail. loud or aggressive campers are not welcome. please note: you must have a weekend pass to camp. three indiviual day passes combined will not enable you to camp.

campsite restictions:

  • campsites are limited to 6 persons and assigned an id if you have more then 6 campers with you-you will be asked to purchase and register an second site at the camping box office.
  • only one tent or camping unit is permitted per camping ticket.
  • all sleeping structures and shelters must display an id numbered camping tag or they will be removed
  • purchase your camping online and register that ticket number and all members of your camping party in advance.
  • if you rent a trailer and have company drop it off your only option for camping is in the dove creek camping area. all rentals are dropped off to an assigned area ahead of time, i.e before the early arrivals program begins.
  • as we are not a provincal camp-ground and do not have numbered sites, however we can offer the chance to purchase within 1 of our three areas through our map
  • campers can register and reserve an adjacent site (bring your own tarps or tape) but they must pay the fee in full and leave thier full names at the camping box office. if you are taking part in the early arrivals program you cannot tarp or tape off adjacent site(s) unless you have paid the early arrival fee for each site. latecomers must supply id to claim thier wristbands. all wristbands must be paid upon inital entry ito the campground whenever an person listed, is present or not.

minors

all ages are warmly welcome; however, minors caming (under 19 yrs of age)must accompanied and supervised by an adult aged 25 or older with a legal gaurdian in addition, there is a limit of 4 minors per adult who will be responsible for the actions of the minors under thier care. underage campers cannot register to camp or recive a wristband without them being accompanied by an adult. that adult must be camping with them for the weekend.we reserve the right to ask for proof of age, bring your id. aggressive, loud or disrespectful behaviorgiving an minor alcohol will not be tolerated disregard for these guidelines will result in you being asked to leave (or removed by security or rcmp) from the campground.

services

drinking water stations are set up throughout the campground. this is an 'unserviced campgroun' as in you'll have to use the services throughout yourself no rv hookups will be provided. garbage, recycling, porta-potties, wash stations for dishes and potable water are avalible at central locations please use the facilites provided on site do not put any human waste or throw dirty water/food water in the bushes. Please do not use any soaps or cleaning products in the pristine salmon-bearing river!
