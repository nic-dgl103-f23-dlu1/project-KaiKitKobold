camping

musicfest camping is sold out.

there is space nearby at puntledge river rv park

steps needed for on-site camping

  1. purchase weekend pass. you must have a weekend pass to camp.
  2. purchase camping pass
  3. a link to the camping registration will be emailed to you with your camping tickets. please follow the instuctions on the registration form and complete all the info required. you cannot register until you have purchased a camping pass.
  4. read all the rules and regulations on this page before proceeding.

why you should camp with us

one of the best ways to truly experience vancouver island musicfest is to fully immerse yourself in the weekend through camping on-site.
familes and friends flock from the local community and far away for an annual reunion of camping and festival fun. our venue is situated on a pastoral acreage bordering the forested banks of the tsoulm river. we provide camping opprotunites for rv's, campers, and camper vans and tents.
this 'rustic camping' environment welcomes festival-goers and gentle jammers who are willing to be respectful, peaceful, and neighbourly
please note: you must have a weekend pass to camp. three indiviual day passes combined will not enable you to camp.

2023 general camping info:

the campground for the early arrivals will open at wendsday, july 12th at 3:00 pm for early arrivals

if you choose to participate in the early arrivals program you will be charged an additional $75+tax for the cost of campground services and security.

the regular arrivals program starts thursday, july 13th at 10 am

regular camping prices apply.

acessible camping sites are avalible:

acessible camping sites are avalible in the inner field area on a first come first serve basis. you must have a valid sparc card or acess 2 number to purchase this area if you do have this identification please contact the office during business hours at:

250-334-1634 acessibility information

campsite restictions:

  • campsites are limited to 6 persons and assigned an id if you have more then 6 campers with you-you will be asked to purchase and register an second site at the camping box office.
  • only one tent or camping unit is permitted per camping ticket.
  • all sleeping structures and shelters must display an id numbered camping tag or they will be removed
  • purchase your camping online and register that ticket number and all members of your camping party in advance.
  • if you rent a trailer and have company drop it off your only option for camping is in the dove creek camping area. all rentals are dropped off to an assigned area ahead of time, i.e before the early arrivals program begins.
  • as we are not a provincal camp-ground and do not have numbered sites, however we can offer the chance to purchase within 1 of our three areas through our map
  • campers can register and reserve an adjacent site (bring your own tarps or tape) but they must pay the fee in full and leave thier full names at the camping box office. if you are taking part in the early arrivals program you cannot tarp or tape off adjacent site(s) unless you have paid the early arrival fee for each site. latecomers must supply id to claim thier wristbands. all wristbands must be paid upon inital entry ito the campground whenever an person listed, is present or not.

camping placement is first come first serve, however through the ticket booking process you can ensure placment within areas that you have paid for.

minors

all ages are warmly welcome;
however, minors (under 19 yrs of age)must be accompanied and supervised by an adult aged 25 or older with a legal gaurdian.
in addition, there is a limit of 4 minors per adult who will be responsible for the actions of the minors under thier care.
underage campers cannot register to camp or recive a wristband without them being accompanied by an adult. that adult must be camping with them for the weekend.
we reserve the right to ask for proof of age, bring your id. aggressive, loud or disrespectful behavior is not tolerated
giving an minor alcohol is not tolerated
disregard for these guidelines will result in you being asked to leave (or removed by security or rcmp) from the campground.

services

drinking water stations are set up throughout the campground. this is an 'unserviced campground' as in you'll have to use the services throughout yourself no rv hookups will be provided. garbage, recycling, porta-potties, wash stations for dishes and potable water are avalible at central locations please use the facilites provided on site do not put any human waste or throw dirty water/food water in the bushes. Please do not use any soaps or cleaning products in the pristine salmon-bearing river!
